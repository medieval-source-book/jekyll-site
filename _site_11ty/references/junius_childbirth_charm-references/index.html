Wood, Diana. Women and Religion in Medieval England . Oxford: Oxbow Books, 2003.

Olsan, Lea. “Latin Charms of Medieval England: Verbal Healing in a Christian Oral Tradition.” Oral Tradition , vol. 7, no. 1, 1992, pp. 116-42.

This paper provides a detailed analysis of childbirth charms and prayers and a discussion of the differences between them. Jones, Peter Murray, and Lea T Olsan. “Performative Rituals for Conception and Childbirth in England, 900-1500.” Bulle- tin of the History of Medicine, vol. 89, no. 3, 2015, pp. 406-33.

This paper focuses on the performative aspect of childbirth prayers and explores the uses of chanting to aid childbirth.
